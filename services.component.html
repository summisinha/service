Personal Insurance

Peraonal insurance purchased for personal or family protection purposes as contrasted with insurance of business property or interests.

Vehicle Insurance

Vehicle insurance will offer you to cover for loss or damage to any vehicle like car, two-wheeler or commercial vehicle, etc.

Property Insurance

Property insurance is a broad term for a series of policies that provide either property protection coverage or liability coverage for property owners.

Health Insurance

Health insurance is a contract that requires an insurer to pay some or all of a person's healthcare costs in exchange for a premium.

Marine Insurance

Marine insurance offers you to provide coverage against any damage/loss caused to cargo vessels, ships, terminals, etc.

Liability Insurance

Liability insurance is defined as a form of insurance that provides protection from third-party lawsuits, if you are liabel.

Fire Insurance

Fire insurance is a contract of insurance against the loss/damage by accidental fire or other occurrences customarily included under a fire policy.

Social Insurance

social insurance, public insurance program that provides protection against various economic risks and in which participation is compulsory.
